An application that enables personalised virtual clothing changes

Project Background

Since the outbreak, mobile phone location has become an important means of controlling the movement of people and investigating close contacts.China is a large country with a large population and several highly populated cosmopolitan cities, which means that the number of people who are present at the same base station with a close caller is enormous.

Problem to Solve

The use of a single, overly simplistic database to store this user signalling data would result in high latency, low security, inefficiency, susceptibility to data corruption or loss, high construction and maintenance costs, and insufficient storage space.Hadoop-architectured systems are ideally suited for this type of scenario.
